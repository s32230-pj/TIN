Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso Díaz, born 29 July 1981 is a Spanish racing driver, currently competing in Formula One for Aston Martin.

Osiągnięcia

Alonso has won two Formula One World Drivers' Championship titles, which he won in 2005 and 2006 with Renault.
In endurance racing, Alonso won the 2018–19 FIA World Endurance Championship and is a two-time winner of the 24 Hours of Le Mans with Toyota,
and remains the only driver to have won both the World Drivers' Championship and the World Endurance/Sportscar Championship.
